# Startup and Business Skills

This category contains Claude skills for startup strategy, financial planning, market research, team building, and competitive analysis. These skills are designed for founders, analysts, and business leads who need Claude to produce structured, investor-grade outputs across the full lifecycle of an early-stage company.

---

## Skills in This Category

### startup-analyst

**File:** [startup-analyst/SKILL.md](./startup-analyst/SKILL.md)
**Lines:** 324

A full-spectrum startup analysis skill covering market sizing, financial modeling, competitive positioning, and strategic planning for early-stage companies. It acts as a senior business analyst advising on the overall viability and direction of a startup.

Activate when you need a broad strategic assessment of a startup idea, want to pressure-test a business model, or need structured analysis across multiple dimensions at once.
